ABOUT

I’m Veishnu K, an architecture student driven by curiosity, experimentation, and the idea that design is never a fixed answer—only an evolving process.

My journey so far has been shaped by exploration across scales—from conceptual narratives to detailed execution. My internship at Matharoo Associates refined how I approach design—learning to question, iterate, and translate ideas into buildable realities.

My work focuses on creating meaningful spatial experiences. Whether it’s a multi-faith center rooted in nature, a fashion hub inspired by movement, or performance spaces influenced by culture, I defy Limits and aim to blend concept, emotion, and function into a cohesive architectural language.

Beyond architecture, I explore motion, speed, and visual storytelling—constantly expanding how I think about space and design. A curious generalist at heart, I explore across disciplines—each shaping how I think, design, and evolve.
